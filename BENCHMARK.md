# Mini Benchmark: Before vs After Pragmatic Guidelines

Use this benchmark to validate that writing quality translates into operational outcomes.

## Core Metrics

### 1. Response Rate

Use for recruiting outreach, stakeholder asks, and cross-team requests.

Formula:
`response_rate = replies / messages_sent`

Recommended windows:
- Recruiting: 7 days
- Internal stakeholders: 5 business days

### 2. Approval Speed

Use for decision-oriented artifacts (PRDs, memos, launch approvals).

Formula:
`approval_speed_days = approval_timestamp - first_share_timestamp`

Lower is better.

### 3. PRD Clarification Rate

Use to measure document clarity at handoff.

Formula:
`prd_clarification_rate = clarification_questions / prd_sections`

Alternative formula:
`clarification_meetings_per_prd`

Lower is better.

## Data Capture Template

Track weekly in a simple table:

| Week | Messages Sent | Replies | Response Rate | Avg Approval Speed (days) | PRD Clarification Rate |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| Baseline-1 | 25 | 3 | 12.0% | 4.5 | 0.82 |
| Baseline-2 | 24 | 2 | 8.3% | 4.1 | 0.75 |
| After-1 | 26 | 5 | 19.2% | 2.8 | 0.48 |
| After-2 | 23 | 5 | 21.7% | 2.2 | 0.34 |

## Minimal Experimental Design

1. Capture at least 2 baseline weeks before applying guidelines.
2. Apply guidelines consistently for 2 to 4 weeks.
3. Keep channel and audience mix comparable.
4. Compare baseline average vs post-change average.
5. Record confounders (seasonality, org changes, hiring freeze).

## Interpretation Rules

- Response Rate: meaningful if +5 percentage points or more.
- Approval Speed: meaningful if reduced by 25% or more.
- PRD Clarification Rate: meaningful if reduced by 30% or more.

## Reporting Snippet

Use this in weekly update:

"After applying pragmatic guidelines for 2 weeks, response rate increased from 10.2% to 20.4%, approval speed dropped from 4.3 to 2.4 days, and PRD clarification rate decreased from 0.79 to 0.41."
